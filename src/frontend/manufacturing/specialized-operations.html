Specialized Manufacturing Operations

info

Zervi Group's manufacturing capabilities include specialized operations customized for textile-based products. The system provides dedicated tracking for Laminating, Cutting, Sewing, and Embroidery with operation-specific parameters and quality metrics.

layers Laminating Operations

Bonding of multiple layers of fabric, foam, or other materials using heat and pressure to create composite materials with specific properties.

Operation Parameters

  • Temperature Range: 120°C - 180°C
  • Pressure Range: 1.5 - 3.5 bar
  • Speed Range: 1 - 4 m/min
  • Layer Support: 2 - 4 layers
